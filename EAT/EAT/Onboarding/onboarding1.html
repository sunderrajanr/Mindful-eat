Why?

  • Many people overeat, diet a lot (restrict what they eat), or just feel badly about their eating.
  • This app can help you feel better about the way you eat and manage your weight.
  • Food-based diets don’t work for most people. You feel deprived or get tired of the diet foods and can’t stick with it.
  • Appetite-monitoring is DIFFERENT. You eat what you want and limit amounts.
